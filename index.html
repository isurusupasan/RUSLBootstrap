Climatic Changes

The rapid change of the climate is a vital issue that we need to address but are at an utter failure through the years. Global warming is known to be the defining issue of rapid climate change. Addressing the key facts resulting in global warming was never a global success.

Food Security and Modern Farming

Number of hungry people all over the world is more than 820 million and ending hunger in the years to come will be challenging if not acted on sprightly.

Pharmaceuticals

A chemical, whenever is combined in a proper ratio, consumed only the necessary, can be medicine unless poisoned. The world is in a habit of unprescribed gulping of antibiotics which is forecasted to lose man’s immunity faster than saving it.
